Biography

I am a conjoint PhD Student at Data61, CSIRO and School of Computer Science and Engineering, The University of New South Wales. My research interests are mainly in the area of storage systems. I am also interested in the advance of machine learning techniques. Currently, I work with Prof. Liming Zhu , Dr. Chen Wang and Dr. Jessie Yin on using learning theory to guide the construction of robust computer systems.
